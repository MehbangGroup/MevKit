In order to import "TestCubeMonitor.json" in to STM32CubeMonitor, please do the following:

1) Open STM32CubeMonitor.
2) In the top right of STM32CubeMonitor, click on the sqare button (with three blue flat lines icon) in the right of the "Deploy" and "Dashboard" buttons.
3) Click "Import".
4) Choose "Clipboard" in the selector at the left side of the page.
5) Click "select a file to import".
6) Navigate to and select "TestCubeMonitor.json".
7) Enjoy!
